InterAction

An app concept to help bring people together without getting rid of their phones.

See how it was made

Step 1: Research & Analysis

Me and 2 other students were tasked with coming up with a solution to fix the problem of people spending too much time on their phones around others.

Problem

A study by Emily Drago shows that the advent of smart technology has negatively impacted the way people communicate. Even when people are aware of the decrease in face-to-face communication due to technology, a majority of them still use mobile devices in the presence of others.

Ideas

After reviewing the case study, my group researched the main points behind this study, developed 3 user personas to work off of, and came up with several ideas to combat this issue, including a cash-for phones program and an app that deletes other apps if you use them for too long.

Solution

After careful deliberation, we decided to create a concept for a mobile app that would allow users to use their phones to provide topics for interesting or meaningful conversations, effectively solving the communication problem without making people put away their phones.
